Connie Li


Connie Li is currently a court clerk at Queens County Supreme Court. She was born and raised in Hong Kong. At the age of 15, she migrated to the U.S. with her parents and three sisters. She took her oath to become an American citizen in 1990.

Although she completed an A.A.S. degree in Computer Programming & a B.A. degree in Economics, her careers never landed on those professional fields. Instead, she taught a NYC public school for six years before she entered the NYS Unified Court System. Since then, she has been a court clerk at Queens County Supreme Court for almost 19 years.

At her leisure time, Connie enjoys brisk walking, jogging, listening to music and go-kart racing with her two grown-up kids. She also has a stamp collection, which has been her main hobby since she was eight years old.
