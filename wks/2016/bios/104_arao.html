Track 1: Within


Dr. Arun Rao


Dr. Arun Rao is currently a Senior Software Engineer at Pixar Animation Studios, and has held various other research, production and software development positions in his 23-year tenure there. Broadly speaking, his work at Pixar entails designing and improving tools to extend artistic reach, and making the movie-making process more efficient. He obtained a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University in 1990, and worked as a Senior Research Scientist at Eastman Kodak Company before joining Pixar.

Outside work, Arun is an avid badminton, photography and music enthusiast, with enthusiasm greatly exceeding talent. His 15 seconds of fame came recently from a voice part in Pixar’s Oscar-nominated short film, “Sanjay’s Super Team”.
