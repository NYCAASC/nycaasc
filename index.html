Come climb with us!

We’re excited to announce that NYCAASC is teaming up with The Cliffs at LIC to bring you a fun night of chilling and climbing!! Come hang out with the coolest Asian American folks in New York City :]

Important: Before you climb, you must fill out this waiver. (You can do it when you get to The Cliffs at LIC, but you can also do it online to save time.) IF YOU ARE A MINOR, YOUR LEGAL GUARDIAN MUST FILL OUT THE FORM FOR YOU.

What is this event?

On Friday, April 7th, come climb to the sky at The Cliffs at a discounted rate! All proceeds will go towards making NYCAASC 2017 an amazing experience for our wonderful attendees!

How much is a pass?

Admission + gear rental is only $20 (normally a $42 value!).

In addition to this deal, all attendees will automatically have the chance to win some gear from Arc'teryx Soho and will have the opportunity to enter a raffle to win $2000 worth of Arc’teryx gear! Be sure to follow our pals at Arc’teryx Soho on Facebook and Twitter.

Cliffs members - be sure to stop by for a special treat at this time ;]

Who can come?

Everyone! This event is open to all ages, ethnicities, and climbing levels.

The New York City Asian American Student Conference

NYCAASC is a student-run, intercollegiate organization dedicated to bringing together students from all around New York City to learn about social issues regarding the Asian/Pacific Islander American community.
