About Us

The New York City Asian American Student Conference is an annual day-long conference built by students, for students, to promote dialogue about racial and sociopolitical issues affecting Asian Americans.

Every year, students from colleges and high schools across New York City come together in a welcoming environment to create solidarity and initiative both locally and beyond.
