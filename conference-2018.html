The 12th Annual New York City Asian American Student Conference: Emergence

Join us for the 12th year of the New York City Asian American Student Conference, a student-run, intercollegiate conference for Asian/Pacific Americans on global, local, and national scales.

What to expect:
  • FREE full day of workshops!
  • Awesome performers!
  • Tons of cool AAPI people to meet :)
  • Free lunch and dinner! (not just pizza)!
  • When
    Saturday, April 28 11am-8pm
  • Where
